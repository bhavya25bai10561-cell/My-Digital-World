Responsible use of social media

Checklist of Do's and Don'ts

This checklist is designed for college students to ensure professional
and safe use of social media.

v Do's

**☑** Give access to those who you know.

**☑** Think before you post.

**☑** Verify the source of information before sharing it.

**☑** Keep your privacy settings high and review them daily.

**☑** Be respectful in comments and discussions.

v Don'ts

**☒** Don't give access to strangers.

**☒** Don't post sensitive information, photos and videos.

**☒** Don't share your passwords with friends or anyone.

**☒** Don't engage in cyber arguments when you are angry.

**☒** Don't over-tag people in posts without their permission.
