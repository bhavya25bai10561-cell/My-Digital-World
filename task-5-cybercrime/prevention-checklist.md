Stay Safe Online

Here we designed \'Stay Safe Online\' Checklist for college students in
India.

\'Stay Safe Online\' Checklist

-   **Use strong and unique passwords:** Create complex passwords by
    mixing with alphabets, numbers and symbols with atleast 8
    characters.
-   **Never click on unknown links:** Unknown links may lead to the
    installation of malwares and hack your devices.
-   Secure your social media: Keep your private settings high and review
    your social media control rgularly.
-   **Never share your UPI PIN/OTP:** UPI PIN is only required to send money
    not to receive it. Never share your OTP/PIN with anyone claiming to
    be from a bank or customer care.
-   **Verify UPI Ids before paying:** Before sending money double-check the
    receiver\'s name.
-   **Avoid using public computers and Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks:** Never
    log to your bank account or enter credit card details in public
    computers and Wi-Fis like cafe.
-   **Think before post:** Don\'t post your private photos and information
    on social media.
-   **Don\'t believe in unofficial online/calls of police and court:** These
    online gevernment officers wear costumes as social proof to make you
    believe.

Official Reporting Channels

If you or someone you know falls victim to a cybercrime, report it
immediately through these official Government of India channels:

-    **National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal:**
    [https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in/)
-   **National Helpline Number:** 1930 (Available 24/7)
